Why I Love Tom Hanks

Tom Hanks is my favorite actor. He has had a long and impressive career playing everything from a boy trapped in an adult body in "Big", to a battle-hardened soldier in "Saving Private Ryan". I think my favorite part he played was as an AIDS patient in the critically-acclaimed movie "Philadelphia", for which he won both a Golden Globe and an Oscar. Tom Hanks' Family

Tom Hanks has been married to Rita Wilson since 1988. Before that, he was married to Samantha Lewes. Together, he and Samantha had two children, Colin and Elizabeth. With Rita Wilson, he has two sons, Chet and Truman Theodore.
